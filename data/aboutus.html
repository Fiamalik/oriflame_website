ABOUT ORIFLAME

While our vision is to be the #1 Beauty Company Selling Direct, our mission has always been to fulfil dreams, for people all over the world. YOUR DREAMS – OUR INSPIRATION Oriflame’s brand promise of Your Dreams - Our Inspiration™ is deeply rooted in our history and heritage. During our 45-year history, we have always been dedicated to inspire and offer people the opportunity to achieve their dreams through our unique business concept. Your Dreams - Our Inspiration™ is a manifestation of our commitment to our clients and consultants; their dreams play a large role in developing our new beauty products and inspiring programmes that help to develop their own business.
